# Milo — Product Hunt Launch Plan
*Created: 2026-03-09*

## Positioning

**One-liner:** Milo replaces your $600/month AI receptionist subscription with a one-time purchase you actually own.

**Tagline options (under 60 chars):**
1. "Own your AI receptionist. No monthly fees. Ever."
2. "AI phone answering you buy once and own forever"
3. "The AI receptionist that doesn't charge rent"

**Narrative angle:** Constraint + Problem-first hybrid
> "Every AI receptionist charges $50-$600/month forever. We built Milo because small businesses shouldn't rent their phone system — they should own it. $399 once. Your infrastructure. Your data. Done."

**Positioning worksheet:**
```
We help small businesses (dental, legal, HVAC, vet, CPA) answer every call without hiring staff or paying monthly AI subscriptions.
The moment they feel value is when their first missed call gets answered and booked automatically.
We are different because you buy once ($399) and own your AI agent — no subscriptions, no per-minute fees, no vendor lock-in.
```

## Why This Angle Wins on Product Hunt

PH loves:
- ✅ Anti-SaaS / ownership models (indie hacker DNA)
- ✅ Clear underdog vs. incumbents story
- ✅ Specific pricing that's shockingly low vs. alternatives
- ✅ Real small business impact (not another dev tool)
- ✅ "Built for" specificity (verticals, not everyone)

The entire AI receptionist market is subscription-based ($49-$4,000/mo). We're the only ownership play under $2,000. That's a PH-ready story.

## Launch Day Assets

### Maker Comment (first comment)
```
Hey Product Hunt! I'm [Sam/owner name] and I built Milo.

Quick backstory: I watched small business owners — dentists, lawyers, HVAC contractors — pay $200-$600/month for AI receptionists that they could never actually own. If they stopped paying, everything disappeared. Their call data, their trained AI, their workflows — gone.

That felt wrong. So we built Milo: an AI phone answering agent you buy once ($399) and own forever. It deploys on your infrastructure. No monthly fees. No per-minute charges. No vendor lock-in.

Right now Milo handles:
- Answering calls 24/7 with natural conversation
- Booking appointments directly into your calendar
- Qualifying leads and routing emergencies
- Industry-specific workflows (dental, legal, HVAC, vet, CPA)

We're a small team and this is our first PH launch. Would love your honest feedback — especially:
1. Does the ownership model resonate, or do people prefer subscriptions?
2. What other verticals should we build for next?
3. What integrations matter most to you?

Thanks for checking it out 🙏
```

### FAQ (for comments)
- **Who is this for?** Small businesses that get 10-200+ calls/day: dental practices, law firms, HVAC companies, vet clinics, accountants.
- **What does it cost?** $399 one-time for a single agent. $2,499 for a full team (phone + email + scheduling + follow-up). No monthly fees.
- **How is this different from Smith.ai / Ruby / My AI Front Desk?** They all charge monthly ($49-$4,000/mo). You're renting. With Milo, you buy once and own it. Over 12 months, Milo saves $500-$45,000+ vs. competitors.
- **What's the catch?** No catch. One-time purchase. You own the agent. We offer optional support plans but the agent runs independently.
- **Can I try it first?** Yes — 7-day free trial, no credit card required.
