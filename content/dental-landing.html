The After-Hours Problem

Most dental practices close at 5pm. But patients don't stop calling at 5pm.

A patient has a toothache at 7pm. They call your office. Voicemail. They call the practice down the road. Someone answers. They book there.

This happens 10-15 times per month at a typical practice. At $200+ per appointment, that's real money walking out the door.

What Your AI Receptionist Does

An AI agent trained on your practice that handles patient calls like your best front desk staff.

  • 01
    Answers every call, 24/7
    Patients hear a natural voice that knows your practice — your services, your hours, your insurance policies. Not a generic recording.
  • 02
    Books appointments into your calendar
    The AI checks your availability, finds the right slot, and confirms with the patient. You get a text summary with the booking details.
  • 03
    Handles insurance and FAQ questions
    "Do you take Delta Dental?" "What are your hours?" "How much is a cleaning?" — trained on YOUR answers, not generic responses.
  • 04
    Flags emergencies
    Severe pain, swelling, trauma — the AI recognizes urgency and forwards critical calls to your personal number immediately.

    Texts you structured summaries
    Every call gets summarized: patient name, reason for calling, urgency level, what was booked or requested. No more listening to voicemails.
