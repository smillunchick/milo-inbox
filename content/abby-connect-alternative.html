What 50 Minutes Actually Looks Like

Abby Connect's cheapest AI plan gives you 50 minutes for $99/month. Here's the reality for a typical service business:

Average call length: 2–3 minutes

That gives you: 17–25 calls per month. About 1 call per business day.

Week 1: You get 8 calls (24 minutes used). Things look fine.

Week 2: 7 more calls. You've used 45 of your 50 minutes. It's the 10th of the month.

Weeks 3–4: Every call costs ~$1.98/min in overages. A 3-minute call = $5.94 extra. Your $99 plan becomes $150, $200, $250.

Or you upgrade: $165/mo for 100 minutes. $299/mo for 200 minutes. $690/mo for 500 minutes. The "affordable AI plan" isn't so affordable anymore.

Per-Minute Billing Punishes Growth

Here's the fundamental problem with per-minute pricing: the more successful your business gets, the more you pay. More customers calling = more minutes = bigger bills. Your AI receptionist should be reducing costs, not scaling them up with revenue.
