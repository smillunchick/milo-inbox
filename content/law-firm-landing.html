LAND:001.2 // the intake problem

The Intake Problem Every Firm Has

Potential clients don't call during business hours. They call when something happens — at 11pm after a DUI arrest, at 6am after discovering their spouse filed for divorce, on Saturday afternoon after a slip-and-fall at a restaurant.

If nobody picks up, they don't leave a voicemail. They call the next firm on Google. By Monday morning, that case belongs to someone else.

According to the ABA, 35% of potential clients who can't reach a firm on their first try never call back. For a PI firm, that's not a missed call — it's a missed $15,000 to $100,000 case.
