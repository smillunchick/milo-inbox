LAND:002.2 // the tax season phone problem

The Tax Season Phone Problem

Every CPA firm knows the pattern. January through April, call volume explodes. Your team is buried in returns, K-1s, and extension requests. And the phone won't stop ringing.

Every time a preparer picks up the phone, they lose 15-20 minutes of focused work. Every call that goes to voicemail risks losing a new client — or frustrating an existing one who just needs to know what documents to bring.

Most firms solve this by hiring a seasonal temp. But training someone who leaves in April is expensive, inconsistent, and exhausting. And the temp can't answer calls at 9pm when a panicked client realizes they're missing a W-2.
