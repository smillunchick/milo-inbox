The Real Cost of Goodcall

Goodcall markets "unlimited minutes" — and that's true. But they cap unique customers per month, and the overages add up fast.

The Customer Cap Trap

Goodcall's Starter plan allows 100 unique customers per month. Their Growth plan allows 250. Every customer beyond the cap costs $0.50 extra.

For a service business, a "unique customer" is anyone with a unique phone number who calls and talks to your agent. New patients, existing patients calling to reschedule, leads asking questions — they all count.

Hidden Cost Example: Growing Dental Practice

A dental practice with 180 unique callers/month on the Starter plan ($79/mo):

$79 + (80 overage customers × $0.50) = $119/mo

Upgrade to Growth ($129/mo) to get 250 customers? Fine — until you hit 300 callers:

$129 + (50 × $0.50) = $154/mo

And that's per agent. Need a second agent for a second location? Double it.
