Hidden Costs to Watch For

⚠️ The per-call/per-minute trap

Providers advertising "$210/month" for 20 calls sound affordable — until your business gets 100+ calls/month. At $9-12/call overage, that "$210/month" service actually costs $930-1,170/month. Always calculate based on your actual call volume, not the base price.

Common hidden costs across providers:

  • Setup/onboarding fees: $50-500 (many charge this on top of monthly)
  • After-hours surcharges: 20-50% premium for nights/weekends
  • Holiday fees: Some charge 2x rates on holidays
  • Integration fees: CRM, calendar, or Zapier connections: $20-100/month extra
  • Call recording storage: Some charge for keeping recordings beyond 30 days
  • Number porting: $25-50 to transfer your existing phone number
  • Contract commitments: Some offer lower rates only with annual contracts (try canceling early)
