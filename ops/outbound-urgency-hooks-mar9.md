# Outbound Urgency Hooks — March 2026
*Supplement to outbound-pipeline-v1.md. Use these as {personalization_hook} inserts or subject line variants.*

## Hook 1: Intuit × Anthropic (CPA/Accounting Vertical)
**Context:** Intuit + Anthropic announced Feb 24 they're building custom AI agents for mid-market businesses via Claude Agent SDK. Rolling out spring 2026.

**For Template B (COO/VP Ops at CPA firms):**

> Intuit just announced they're building AI agents for accounting firms — built into QuickBooks, locked to their platform. By the time it rolls out (likely fall), firms that moved independently will be 6 months ahead.

**Subject line variant:** `{company}'s AI strategy before Intuit decides it for you`

**Why it works:** Creates urgency without fear-mongering. Positions Milo as the independent choice vs. vendor lock-in.

## Usage Notes
- Use ONE hook per email, not multiple
- The Intuit hook is highest-signal for CPA/accounting firms specifically
- The "agentic AI" hook works for any vertical
- Always personalize beyond the hook — reference something specific about the person or company
- These hooks supplement, don't replace, the personalization_hook field in templates
